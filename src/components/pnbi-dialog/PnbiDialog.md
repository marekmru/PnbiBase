### Usage

Starter Element for Dialogs with convenience functions

```jsx
<pnbi-dialog width="1440px" title="Dialog Header">
  <div slot="dialog-content">What can i do you for?</div>
  <div slot="dialog-actions">
    <v-btn dark color="primary" flat>
      Close
    </v-btn>
    <v-btn dark color="primary" flat>
      Ok
    </v-btn>
  </div>
</pnbi-dialog>
```
